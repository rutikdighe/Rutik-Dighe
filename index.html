22 Years Of Farming Tradition

HISTORY

Since the year 2000 Satral Dairy has been producing pure milk and delivering it to millions of houses in Maharashtra and Gujarat. Satral Dairy was established by Mr. Dilip Sakharam gagare, the dairy got its name from the native village known as “Satral”. The business began on a small scale, with all operations centred in Satral and neighbouring areas to the village. Today the dairy has a fully operational milk processing machine capable of producing 7000 litres of milk per day. Overall 3000 milk vendors travel to Satral Dairy to collect milk.
